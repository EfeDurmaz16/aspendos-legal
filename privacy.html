Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all iOS applications published by Aspendos ("we", "our", "us"), including:

QuickExpenseSolo PDFMarkPro FastTrack VoiceMemoAI InvoiceSnap BabyLog QRSnapPro PlantMate

Our Core Principle

Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you create within our apps is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage technologies.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing or search history
  • Health or fitness data

On-Device Data Storage

Our apps store data locally on your device to provide their core functionality. This includes content you create such as expenses, invoices, notes, schedules, feeding logs, scanned codes, and plant records. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • Protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Automatically included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled

Device Permissions

Some of our apps may request access to device features. These permissions are used only for in-app functionality and data never leaves your device:

  • Camera (QRSnapPro, PlantMate): Used to scan QR/barcodes or capture plant photos. Images are processed and stored locally.
  • Photo Library (InvoiceSnap, PlantMate): Used to select images for invoices or plant identification. Selected images are stored locally within the app.
  • Contacts (QRSnapPro): Used optionally to save scanned contact information. Access is read/write only when you explicitly choose to save a contact.

You can revoke any permission at any time through your device's Settings app.

In-App Purchases

Our apps may offer in-app purchases (one-time lifetime unlock) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no data from any user, our apps are safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Deleting content within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all app data)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
